About this game

Derelict Station Breachline is a turn-based tactical survival run. Sectors are procedurally generated, enemy placement shifts by level, and successful runs depend on route planning instead of random rushing.

How to play

Select a sector, deploy, and move one tile per turn toward extraction. After every move, enemies react and close distance, so movement efficiency is the core skill.

Controls

  • Move: WASD or Arrow keys, plus swipe on touch devices.
  • Run controls: R to retry, N/P for sector navigation, Esc to pause.
  • Tactical toggles: fog, grid hints, touch confirmation, sound, and motion effects.

Objective

Reach the extraction beacon before hostiles collapse your route. You can chase better move/time records, but survival comes first.

Tips & strategy

  • Prefer corridors over open rooms to reduce enemy approach angles.
  • Use the minimap and event log to plan two to three turns ahead.
  • Reset quickly on a bad opening sector to preserve run rhythm.

FAQ

Is this game replayable in short sessions?
Yes. Runs are designed to start fast, resolve quickly, and encourage repeat attempts.

Does progress save online?
No. Best results are saved only in your browser storage per sector.

Can I play on mobile?
Yes, with swipe movement and optional touch confirmation for safer input.
